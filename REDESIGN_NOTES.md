# Qorvexa Luxury Redesign

This redesign applies the selected light-luxury visual direction across the complete Next.js website.

## Design direction

- Ivory and warm-white backgrounds
- Graphite/navy surfaces for strong technical contrast
- Champagne-gold accents used selectively
- Editorial serif display headings with Manrope body typography
- Soft glass cards, restrained borders, and premium shadow depth
- Floating phone and laptop product visual in the home hero
- CSS-only device motion, shimmer, orbit, hover lift, and reveal animation support
- Responsive layouts for desktop, tablet, and mobile

## Redesigned routes

- `/`
- `/about`
- `/products`
- `/products/samira-collection`
- `/how-we-work`
- `/team`
